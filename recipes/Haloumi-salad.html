Halloumi Salad

Halloumi Salad with Brown rice and Vegetables

This Recipe is a nice and healthy 30 minute Dish with a refreshing summer taste

Ingredients

  • Brown Rice 200g
  • Halloumi 200g
  • 1 Zuccini
  • Tomatoes 50g
  • Half a Cucumber
  • Yoghurt 40g
  • Boulione
  • 1 Onion

Steps

  1. Wash the Vegetables and cook the rice with the Boulion in double the water should be half of a finger cup water above the rice
  2. Slice all the vegetables in mouth friendly sizes
  3. fry the zuccini with onion in the pan and season with some salt and pepper
  4. after the zuccini are golden brown set them to the side and fry the Halloumi
  5. Assemble everthying in the bowl season again and put the yoghurt on top
